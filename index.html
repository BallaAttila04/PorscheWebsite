About The Car

The Porsche 930 Turbo, introduced in 1975, is a legendary sports car that redefined high-performance driving. With its distinctive design, including the iconic "whale tail" spoiler, and a powerful turbocharged engine, the 930 became a symbol of 1980s automotive excess. Its 3.3-liter flat-six engine, coupled with a KKK turbocharger, delivered exhilarating performance, reaching 0 to 60 mph in around 5 seconds. The 930 Turbo's influence extends beyond its time, shaping the legacy of the Porsche 911 Turbo series and solidifying Porsche's reputation as a leader in the world of sports cars.
